This page details some additional steps that may be required when deploying Rookout on more advanced environments.

## Allowing Rookout through a firewall

Allowing Rookout components to communicate with the Rookout service backend may require adding an inclusion rule in your firewall. In that case, traffic should be allowed to the domain "control.rookout.com" at port 443.
